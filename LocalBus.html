Frequently Asked Questions

How does the bus alarm alert system work?

Users enter their destination, and the system tracks the bus's location. When the bus is near the station, an alarm notifies the user.

Can I search for local transport like taxis or e-rickshaws?

Yes, our platform allows users to search for nearby smart and local vehicles.

Is the service available in all cities?

Currently, we support select locations, but we are expanding our coverage.

Does the app work offline?

The bus alert requires an internet connection for real-time tracking, but previously searched transport details can be accessed offline.

Do I need to create an account to use the service?

No, basic features like searching for transport can be used without an account, but saving preferences requires registration.
